About SecuriPredict

SecuriPredict is a state-of-the-art decision support system designed to assist event organizers in optimizing security staffing.

Our system utilizes a Random Forest Regressor machine learning model trained on extensive historical datasets encompassing diverse event scenarios. By analyzing complex variables such as crowd dynamics, threat levels, and environmental factors, we provide data-driven recommendations that enhance public safety while maintaining operational efficiency.

Machine Learning Core

We use ensemble learning methods to provide robust predictions that account for non-linear relationships between event factors.

Real-time Risk Analysis

Our risk scoring engine evaluates environmental and historical data to flag potential safety concerns before they arise.
